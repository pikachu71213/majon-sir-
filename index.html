FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can enroll at HIAT? +
Anyone who has completed 10th grade or above can enroll. Our courses cater to students, graduates, working professionals, and business owners looking to upgrade their financial knowledge.
Do you provide placement assistance? +
Yes. We offer 100% placement assistance including interview preparation, resume building, and direct referrals to our network of CA firms, businesses, and tax consultancies in Bhiwani and Haryana.
Are batch timings flexible? +
Absolutely. We offer morning (10–2 PM), afternoon (3–5 PM)
What certificate do I receive? +
On successful completion of any course, you receive an HIAT Certificate of Completion co-signed by the course instructor. This certificate is recognized by businesses and accounting firms throughout Haryana.
Is EMI or installment payment available? +
Yes. We offer easy 2–3 installment payment options for all our courses. Contact us on WhatsApp or visit us in person to discuss a plan that suits you.
